Alex Marvez Biography-Wiki

Alex Marvez is an American sportscaster, journalist, and author born on April 3, 1971, in The Bronx, New York, United States, who currently works for Sirius XM NFL Radio where he hosts a daily prime time radio show.

Marvez is also the Senior NFL Reporter for Fox Sports, and Fox Sports 1, a position he has held since the year 2007. In addition, he is an NFL Insider at SportingNews.com as well as has been a beat writer for the Miami Dolphins, Denver Broncos and Cincinnati Bengals. A former Pro Football Writers of America president, he has covered the NFL since 1995 and has a Hall of Fame vote.

In 2019, he joined the professional wrestling promotion All Elite Wrestling as a play-by-play commentator as well as became a part of the inaugural broadcast team, alongside Jim Ross and Excalibur. As of October 2019, he became the backstage interviewer for AEW.

Marvez has previously written for The Washington Times and has appeared as a guest on various shows including the Geraldo Rivera Report, the Dan Patrick Show as well as Lou Dobbs Tonight. Marvez appears in the year 2011 sports documentary series A Football Life and played himself in the 2014 movie Draft Day.
