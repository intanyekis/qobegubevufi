Amy Robach Reveals TJ Holmes Needed A Welfare Check After The GMA Scandal Broke

Highlights

  • Amy Robach and T.J. Holmes went through a dark period after their affair scandal broke in 2022, with both of them struggling with their mental health.
  • Holmes spiraled into a dark place and sent Robach text messages expressing regret and love, leading Robach to fear for his safety.
  • Both Robach and Holmes were dealing with the aftermath of the scandal in different ways, with Holmes turning to alcohol and drugs while Robach battled negative media portrayals and contemplated suicide.

Amy Robach and T.J. Holmes have been through the wringer this past year. However, it was unclear just how bad things got until Robach recently revealed that Holmes needed a welfare check after the Good Morning America scandal broke in 2022, making it clear how the couples' mental health suffered after their affair was revealed.

On Tuesday, Robach and Holmes came clean about what life was like after their affair scandal broke in 2022 and the true relationship between the two was finally clear. During their podcast, Amy & T.J., Robach recalled how Holmes spiraled.

What Did T.J. Holmes Tell Amy Robach?

She was so concerned about his behavior that Robach called police to perform a welfare check on the man she was in love with.

"We got to a point... where you were in a pretty dark spot," Robach said to Holmes. "A pretty dark space."

The dark space came after Holmes started to text Robach in "past tense" form.

"I'm sorry, you were the love of my life," the text message from Holmes to Robach read. "I'm so sorry this has happened."

Robach went on to explain, "I start to panic. I was so afraid that he had done something."

"When I was sending them to you, I didn't know how I was sounding," Holmes stated. "I'm not sure where my head was at that time."

When Holmes was sending the text messages to Robach, he admitted that the day the scandal broke, once he left work he "immediately started pounding vodka."
