Calvin Ayre

Birthname Calvin Ayre Birthdate May 25, 1961 Birthplace Lloydminster, Canada Education University of Waterloo, City University, Washington Profession Businessman Net worth $1,200,000 Source of Wealth Bodog, Online Nationality United States Country United States

Canadian businessman Calvin Ayre has an estimated net worth of $1.2 million in 2012. He is known to be the founder of the commercial brand and online game, Bodog.

Calvin Ayre was born in Lloydminster, Saskatchewan, Canada on May 25, 1961. He is a graduate of the University of Waterloo where he completed his Bachelor of Sciences (BSc) in General Sciences in 1984. He also studied at City University of Seattle where he took his MBA in Management Finance. In 2006, he was featured as the “Hottest Bachelor” by People Magazine and in late 2007, he was featured on the list of "Most Eligible Billionaire Bachelors” by Star Magazine.

Calvin Ayre is the founder of Calvin Ayre Foundation which was launched in 2005. It is a private and independent organization which seeks to offer millions of pledges to numerous charitable organizations across the globe. Among these organizations include Sean McCauley Hope Foundation for Pediatric Cancer, the LA Lakers Youth Foundation, the Fisher House Foundation, the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation, the Humane Society, and the Animal Avengers Organization.
