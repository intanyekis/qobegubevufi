Calvin Klein Superyacht: A Peek Inside The Fashion Mogul's Luxurious Vessel

Calvin Klein has amassed a $700 million fortune from the fashion label that carries his moniker. The fashion mogul began his career working as an apprentice at a suit manufacturing company before he launched a company with his childhood friend that was later named Calvin Klein Inc., in 1968. Taking care of the artistic vision, Klein launched the now-iconic denim line in the 70s and launched the perfume line a decade later. Gaining critical acclaim and several awards for his work, Klein has garnered a hefty fortune from his work as a successful fashion icon. Klein spends a fortune buying several real estate properties and luxury cars.

Along with owning luxury cars and private aviation, Calvin Klein also likes to set sail in style. He owns a stunning superyacht often seen along the coast of the French Mediterranean and during family vacations. A stunning motorboat creation, let’s take a look at Calvin Klein’s superyacht Vantage.

Created By The Best

One hundred fifty feet of stunning beauty, the Vantage is a sophisticated yet sleek superyacht that truly defines Calvin Klein. The American shipbuilder Palmer Johnson Shipyards built the yacht, as noted by Club Yacht. A century-old company, Palmer Johnson is a well-established name known for creating luxury and high-performing yachts often owned by famous personalities. Having collaborated with big names like Bugatti, the company added Calvin Klein to its list of clientele.

The Vantage was built with elegance by Palmer Johnson, and as a fashion mogul, Calvin Klein ensured that only the best designers were on board to create the interior. Klein commissioned Carlo Nuvolari and Dan Lenard to enhance the elegance. The Italian duo of an architect and interior designer worked closely with Klein using his signature shades of brown, blue, and cream to offer a contemporary ambiance. The duo has previously worked on designing the luxury yacht Alfa Nero and Steven Spielberg’s Seven Seas. Klein, who is appreciative of lighting, ensured that every part of the superyacht allowed ample natural lighting during the day through the large windows and open spaces.

A Luxury Home At Sea

Meeting every luxury standard, the Vantage has occupancy for 12 guests, offering six staterooms and a stunning Master suite. According to Yacht Harbour, the Master Suite features a king-size bed, his and hers bathrooms, and an adjoining study area for Klein to get some work done. The two twin cabins and two double staterooms are also equipped with ensuite facilities to meet every need. The yacht can carry nine members on board and offer ample living space inside the crew quarters. As someone who lives a fit lifestyle, Klein also had a fully-equipped gym on board.

Along with a working kitchen serving gourmet cuisines, Vantage is also equipped with a barbecue to enjoy a day out in the sun. An assortment of drinks is made available at the bar stations around the yacht that can be enjoyed by gazing out from the private deck. In addition to the swimming pool and direct access to the sea, the superyacht has a Jacuzzi for a relaxed spa-like experience, as stated by Money Inc. The masculine interior of brown, blue, and cream is created from fabrics by Rubelli, Nicotrend, and Aura. The teak in the interior is made from Zebrano accents while the tiles are wenge.

Offering a cruising speed of 24 knots and a maximum speed of 28 knots, the Vantage comes with a pair of MTU 12V 400 M90 engines. The tank has a 40,000-liter capacity, covering the range of 1,000 Nm. The superyacht is the ideal vessel for relaxed cruising during the summers as Klein is often seen on vacations with his family and friends on board the Vantage. At anchor, it is equipped with stabilizers that can balance the yacht if rough waters hit the boat.

While there is ample space to relax on various decks and lounges, Calvin Klein has also thrown lavish parties on his yacht. As mentioned by LuxuryLaunches, Klein threw an exclusive party during the opening night of the 2011 Cannes International Film Festival. Super Yacht organized the event wherein the guests arrived from the Hotel Martinez Jetee and were served champagne after arriving on board. Along the backdrop of Cannes, Klein’s party raged on all night until the early hours of sunlight. Several prominent guests from the Film Festival were in attendance, along with the jury members and celebrities.

Calvin Klein’s Vantage perfectly encapsulates luxury at sea and the genius behind the fashion mogul who created the ultimate yacht for parties and leisure. The superyacht has a comforting interior that defines his personality, while the exterior is sleek and modern, raising a new standard for exteriors.
