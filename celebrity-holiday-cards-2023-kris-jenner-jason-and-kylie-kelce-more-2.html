Celebrity Holiday Cards 2023: Kris Jenner, Jason and Kylie Kelce, More

‘Tis the season for mailboxes to be filled to the rim with holiday cards and our favorite celebrities are ushering in the festive spirit by unveiling their 2023 version.

Through the years, the Kardashian-Jenner family has posed for several over-the-top greeting cards – and sometimes they even enlist the help of Photoshop. In 2019, Kim Kardashian admitted to adding daughter North into her card with ex Kanye West after their eldest child wouldn’t cooperate with the photo shoot.

Across the pond, the British royal family are also known for their festive cards. In 2022, Prince William and Princess Kate shared their sweet family snap via Instagram dressed casually alongside their three children, Prince George, Princess Charlotte and Prince Louis. The family all coordinated in shades of blue and held hands walking down a path.
