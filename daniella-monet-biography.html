Daniella Monet Bio (Wiki)

Who is Daniella Monet?

Daniella Monet is an American actress and singer she is famous as Trina Vega in the Nickelodeon sitcom ‘Victorious’.

She also played various guest roles on television. Daniella played an interesting role in the comedy-drama ‘Zoey 101’ and in the movie ‘Nancy Drew’ she had a supporting role.
