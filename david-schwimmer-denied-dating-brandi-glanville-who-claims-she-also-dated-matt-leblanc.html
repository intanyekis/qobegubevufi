Brandi Glanville Claims She Dated A Lot Of Celebrities In Hollywood

During her time on Danny Pellegrino’s “Everything Iconic” podcast, Brandi Glanville opened up about past relationships. The Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills star revealed that she dated a lot around Hollywood. That included a 'week of fun' with actor Gerard Butler. Brandi disclosed about her time with the star, “I was on ‘Housewives’ but he did not know that and it really bothered him that I didn’t tell him that… I don’t lead with, somebody comes up to me at a beach party, ‘Hi, I’m on ‘Housewives’.’ That’s a****le-like.”

She continues, "I dated a lot, I made out a lot. I made out with one of the men in ‘The Notebook’, they were single at the time, that’s all I will say, I can’t say [who],” she gushed. “I dated Ben Stiller for a little while."

Glanville clearly hasn't been afraid to detail her past, and the same held true with her apparent experiences alongside both stars from Friends, David Schwimmer and Matt LeBlanc. Two actors in particular tend to keep things quiet about their romantic lives.

Brandi Glanville Told The Story Of Her Date With Matt LeBlanc, And Her Time With David Schwimmer

Glanville had very different experiences on dates with Matt LeBlanc and David Schwimmer. As for her time with Matt, it only resulted in one date. According to The Real Housewives star, the date took a turn after LeBlanc shared his ice cream with his dog.

Glanville reveals with Page Six, “We went back to his house, he let his dog lick his ice cream and I was out,” she said. “I was like, ‘Nope.’ I was totally gonna have sex with him, then the dog licked the ice cream and he licked the ice cream and I was like, ‘I can’t.'”

As for her time with David Schwimmer, Glanville was bothered by the fact that the actor was wearing makeup during their date.

“He wore makeup,” she said. “He wore concealer out during the day and it really annoyed me. I understand you’re on set, you’re used to wearing makeup, but I wasn’t used to a man wearing makeup. In the moment, it bothered me.”

Matt LeBlanc did not confirm or deny the story. However, David Schwimmer's team were quick to completely deny all allegations and stories told by Glanville pertaining to her time with David. In truth, Schwimmer's relationships struggled behind the scenes due to his surging popularity with Friends.

David Schwimmer Revealed That His Fame On Friends Messed Up His Relationships

Although David Schwimmer became increasingly wealthy, the fame factor negatively impacted his personal life. Due to his Ross character on television, fans assumed the actor was easy to approach, and that tends to be the case for lots of other actors and actresses. However, David reveals that his relationships took a hit, and that he was constantly trying to hide.

“It was pretty jarring and it messed with my relationship to other people in a way that took years, I think, for me to kind of adjust to and become comfortable with. As an actor, the way I was trained, my job was to observe life and to observe other people, and so I used to walk around with my head up, and really engaged and watching people," Global News quotes Schwimmer.

He continues, “The effect of celebrity was the absolute opposite. “It made me want to hide under a baseball cap, not be seen. And I realized after a while that I was no longer watching people; I was trying to hide. So I was trying to figure out: How do I be an actor in this new world, in this new situation? How do I do my job? So that was tricky.”

As for his past relationships, Schwimmer married Zoe Buckman in 2010 and the former couple had a daughter a year later. They split in 2017. It would appear as though the popular sitcom star is currently single.
