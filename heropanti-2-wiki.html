Heropanti 2 Movie Story

RJ is a kind young man who lives in Yorkshire with his mother Hema and works as a bouncer at a nightclub. Inaaya, a self-made billionaire from the gaming sector, encounters RJ and accuses him of being her ex-boyfriend Babloo Ranawat. RJ makes it plain that he is unfamiliar with her. Inaaya's suspicions, however, prove to be correct. Babloo was a well-known hacker who was recruited by CBI officer Azaad Khan, who offered him a lucrative reward in exchange for his assistance in foiling the intentions of Laila, a psychopathic magician, and cyber-criminal.

Laila has created software that takes users' personal information as well as money from their bank accounts. Babloo infiltrates Laila's gang by wooing Laila's sister Inaaya and begins to form a positive impression of her, as well as being lured by the money offered by him, and betrays Azaad, but soon realizes that the latter is achieving to destroy lives when he meets an ambulance driver's wife Hema (Babloo's mother), where he learns that her husband had saved money for his grandson' As a result, the driver took his own life.

Babloo returns to Azaad, subdues the henchmen, and destroys the app's transaction due to remorse. He assumes the alias of RJ and departs with his adopted mother Hema to begin a new life. Laila hears that Babloo is hiding in Yorkshire today and sent his men to find him, but Babloo subdues them and fakes his death once more. Babloo confesses Laila's sins to Inaaya, who joins him in the struggle. Babloo travels to Egypt, Russia, and China, where Laila has set up his crime network and smashes it.

Babloo overcomes a number of obstacles, including defeating Laila and stopping the extraction, before departing with Inaaya and Hema. Laila, humiliated by her defeat, commits herself. Critical Response

Critics panned Heropanti 2, claiming that Tiger deserved something better. India Today's Tanushree Roy gave the film a 3/5 rating and wrote: "Heropanti 2, starring Tiger Shroff, Tara Sutaria, and Nawazuddin Siddiqui, is a paisa vasool entertainment. However, it has problems of its own ". "Despite rich production value and amazing stunts by Tiger Shroff, HEROPANTI 2 suffers from a terrible plot," commented Taran Adarsh of Bollywood Hungama, who gave the film a 3/5 grade. The Times of India's Archika Khurana awarded the film a 2.5/5 rating, writing, "Heropanti 2 provides a little bit of everything—action, drama, music, romance, and exotic settings." "Tiger Shroff doesn't even bother to act in this one," stated a Pinkvilla reviewer, giving the film a 1.5/5 grade. It lacks the heroism, thrill, romance, emotion, humor, and action that a "true commercial picture" should have. Scroll.in's Nandini Ramnath awarded the film a 1/5 rating. The Indian Express' Shubhra Gupta gave the film a 1/5 rating, writing, "This film has no storyline." When Tiger Shroff isn't mowing down hordes of villains, it's simply a succession of set-pieces with him prancing, romancing, and dancing." NDTV's Saibal Chatterjee gave the film a 1/5 rating and wrote: "This is a mashup of a film constructed on a mountain of nonsense that becomes larger by the minute. Neither Tiger nor Nawazuddin will be able to save it ".. "They say certain movies leave a nasty aftertaste in your mouth, Heropanti 2 leaves blisters & sores," commented Umesh Punwani of Koimoi, who gave the film a 0.5/5 rating.
