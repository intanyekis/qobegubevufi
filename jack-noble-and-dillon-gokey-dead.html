Incident Details

The incident occurred on a Monday morning around 9 a.m. when a series of collisions took place involving a tractor-trailer, a Honda, and a Hyundai. According to preliminary reports, the tractor-trailer driver collided with a stationary Honda that was waiting to make a turn onto Route 212. The impact caused the tractor-trailer to veer into the westbound lanes, where it collided with a Hyundai carrying three juniors from Kingston High School. The force of the collision was so severe that the tractor-trailer overturned, leading to a subsequent collision with an Audi traveling in the opposite direction.

Tragically, two occupants of the Hyundai, 16-year-old Dillon Gokey from Kingston and 17-year-old Jack Noble from Port Ewen, lost their lives at the scene. Another classmate who was also in the Hyundai sustained serious injuries but is expected to recover. The driver of the Audi miraculously emerged from the crash unharmed.
