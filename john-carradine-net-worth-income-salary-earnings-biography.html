John Carradine Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

John Carradine (February 5, 1906 – November 27, 1988) was an American actor best remembered for his performances in horror films, Westerns, and Shakespearean theatre. He was a member of Cecil B. DeMille’s stock company and subsequently John Ford’s, and was one of Hollywood’s most prolific character performers. He was the patriarch of the Carradine family, which included four of his sons and four of his grandkids who are or were actors. He was married four times, had five children, and was the father of the Carradine family.
