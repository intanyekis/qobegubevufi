Jonathan Antin Wiki Biography

Jonathan Antin was born on the 16th August 1967, in Los Angeles County, California US, and is known to the world as an actor and entrepreneur, but the main source of his net worth comes from his work as a celebrity hair stylist. He has been active since the 1990s.

Have you ever wondered how rich Jonathan Antin is, as of early 2018? According to sources, it has been estimated that Jonathan Antin`s overall net worth is $4 million, an amount acquired through his numerous talents, and business ideas, including selling his own hair product, “Jonathan Product” in the beauty salons Sephora.

Jonathan Antin Net Worth $4 Million

Jonathan grew up in a family with two brothers – Steve Antin, who is a film director and Neil Antin an actor, plus sister Robin Antin, who is a founder of the musical group Pussycat Dolls. Before his professional career began, Jonathan attended the Fairfax Beauty Academy, and soon after graduation became a hair stylist at the West Hollywood salon. He worked there for quite some time, working his way up and increasing his net worth. Jonathan eventually bought the hair salon and renamed it “Jonathan Salon West Hollywood”, where he charged $500 for a haircut. Before long, Jonathan became one of the most popular and sought-after hair stylists, with some of his customers having included Rod Stewart, Tiger Woods, Madonna, Kirsten Dunst, and Kate Bosworth, among others. This has certainly increased Jonathan`s net worth.

All this success enabled him to add a second salon which he opened in Beverly Hills, increasing further his net worth. While in the process of opening of his new salon, Jonathan was featured in the reality TV series “Blow Out” (2004), during the filming of which one of Jonathan`s customer was the famous fitness trainer Jackie Warner.

The same year, Jonathan also featured as a judge of the Miss Teen USA Pageant, and also appeared in an episode of the “Bravo Show”, entitled “Queer Eye For The Straight Guy”, which certainly contributed to his net worth too.

Further on in his successful career on television, he was featured in the TV serial documentary “101 Guiltiest Guilty Pleasures” (2006), and in 2010 he appeared in the TV series “The Biggest Loser”, and as a guest star in the “CSI: Crime Scene Investigations”. The same year, Jonathan served as a judge in the reality TV series “Sheer Genius”, all of which also added to his net worth.

To speak further of his accomplishments, Jonathan has appeared in magazines such as Sense and Inked, and been featured in many others.

His more recent ventures include an appearance in the reality TV series “L.A Hair” (2015), and he is to launch a new hair product, as he canceled production of his past brand, Jonathan Product.

Regarding his personal life, Jonathan was married to boutique owner Sescie Karabuykov from 2006 until 2011, when she filed for divorce; the couple have a son and a daughter.
