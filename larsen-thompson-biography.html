Larsen Thompson Bio (Wiki)

Who is Larsen Thompson?

Larsen Thompson is an American actress, Model, and Dancer by profession.

She is best known for emerging as a backup dancer on the reality shows like The Voice, America’s Got Talent, and The X Factor.

Currently, she is shooting for a reoccurring role in the Netflix series “The Midnight Club”.

Larsen Thompson: Age, Family, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

The actress was born on November 20, 2000, in Thousand Oaks, California, the U.S. Her full name is Larsen Grace Thompson.

Larsen holds an American nationality and is of mixed descent of Irish, English, Scottish, and Norwegian. She is the daughter of Kirk Thompson whereas her mother’s detailed information is still under the curtain.

She has also a brother named Cole Thompson. Furthermore, her aunt, Julianne McNamara is a gymnast who once won an Olympic gold medal.

Education & Early Life

Larsen attended the Newbury Park High School in California. When she was four years old, she joined a dance class, and also she had trained in six different disciplines, including tap, ballet, hip-hop, Contemporary, and more.

At the age of 12, she started traveling to international dance conventions and signed with Zuri Models Children’s talent agency.

Larsen Thompson: Professional Life, Career

Larsen got her first big break when she was fifteen years old when she was featured in the Youtube video ‘IDFWU’ alongside Taylor Hatala. The video went viral which received over 4.7 million views to date.

Before the big break, she had already participated in a different reality show as a backup dancer such as America’s Got Talent, ‘X’ Factor, Shake It Up, and more. In 2016, she was featured in Sia’s music video for ‘The Greatest,’ and in Born’s track ‘American Monkey’.

Larsen has featured in the 2017 official dance video for P! nk’s ‘Beautiful Trauma,’ and Katy Perry’s ‘Chained to the Rhythm.’ Similarly, she has modeled for numerous fashion brands like Dior, Fendi, Juicy Couture, Hollister, Puma, and so on.

Besides, she has appeared in the 2010 American documentary film, ‘I Am’ as a daughter. In 2014 starred in the TV series, Q N’ A with Miki and Shay. Likewise, she appeared in the film, Bloodline along with Seann Willian Scott in 2018.
