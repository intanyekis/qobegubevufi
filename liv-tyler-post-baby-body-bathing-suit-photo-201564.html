Liv Tyler Reveals Post-Baby Body in One-Piece Bathing Suit: Beach Pic

Life's a beach for this new mama!

Almost two months after giving birth to baby boy Sailor Gene, Liv Tyler flaunted her post-baby body in a striped swimming ensemble while vacationing in Miami on Saturday, April 4.

The Lord of the Rings alum, 37, took to the beach in a plunging one-piece bathing suit under a sheer, zebra-print cover-up. For beauty, she wore barely any makeup, though she did rock a crimson manicure and a bright smile.

But Tyler didn't vacation alone—her whole family came along, too! Her boyfriend and the father of her second child, Dave Gardner, stayed by her side throughout the trip. (Tyler also shares 10-year-old son Milo with musician Royston Langdon.) She was also joined by friend Tabitha Simmons for the getaway.

The next day, Easter Sunday, Tyler hopped on Instagram to share a photo of herself and her newborn son, who was clad in a bunny sweater. "Happy Easter. Beautiful family weekend in the sun @davidgardner76 @tabithasimmons love you," she wrote.
