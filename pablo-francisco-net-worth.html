Pablo Francisco

Birthname Pablo Ridson Francisco Birthdate January 5, 1974 Birthplace Tucson, Arizona Profession Comedian Net worth $5,000,000 Source of Wealth Television Nationality United States-Chile Country United States

Originating from Chile, Pablo Francisco is an American comedian with an estimated net worth of $5 million as of 2012.

Joining the cast of MADtv in the 1990's led to the creation of his first stand-up comedy special titled Comedy Central Presents. Together with Freddy Soto and Carlos Mencia, Pablo Francisco went on a tour as part of the "The Three Amigos". As a comedian, he has performed in shows like Mind of Mencia, Frank TV, The Tonight Show with Jay Leno and ILL-ustrated. In the past, he has been character voices and has made effects for many stars like Droopy Dog, Count Dracula, Jimi Hendrix, Chris Rock, R2-D2, Steve Austin 'Stone Cold', Ricky Martin, Ozzy Osbourne, George Clooney, Anthony Sullivan, Mr. Magoo, Aladdin, Dennis Hopper, Kermit the Frog and Christopher Walken.
