Sandra Bullocks Son Louis Lied to Protect Sister Laila

Sandra Bullock’s 8-year-old son Louis has his little sister’s back. When Laila, 6, did something naughty, her big brother did everything he could to make sure she wouldn’t get in trouble.

“She did something — I won’t say what it was — but I tried to get it out of him because he’s very honest,” Bullock, 53, revealed on the Thursday, May 17, episode of The Ellen DeGeneres Show. “And he was like, ‘She didn’t do it. Maybe she did this,’ and kept coming up with difference scenarios.”

Later that night, as the Oscar winner and her longtime photographer boyfriend, Bryan Randall, were putting Louis to bed it was clear his conscious was bothering him. “He’s like, ‘I don’t feel well. I have this tickling in my stomach. I have to tell you something,’” Bullock recalled. “I said, ‘Okay, what is it?’ He goes, ‘I can’t tell you because I just like her so much.’”

Still, the siblings have their battles. “There are moments when they want to kill each other,” quipped the Ocean’s 8 star. According to Bullock, Laila (pronounced Lila) tends to be the aggressor: “Laila is more likely to get irritated with her brother. But I like that about her. I like that she’ll take a knife and cut a guy.”
