Sofia Wylie Photos Of The Star Hollywood Life

Sofia Wylie is a talented young actress. She’s best known for her role as Buffy on the critically-acclaimed Disney Channel series ‘Andi Mack’ alongside Peyton Elizabeth Lee, Joshua Rush, and Asher Angel. She’s also been a dancer since she was 5 years old. Sofia stars as the lead character Cory Bailey in the Australian film ‘Back of the Net,’ which makes its U.S. debut on June 15 on Disney Channel. She will also play Gina in the upcoming Disney+ series ‘High School Musical: The Musical.’ The series is set to debut on Disney’s new streaming service in Nov. 2019. In addition to acting, she debuted her first single, ‘Side to Side,’ for ‘Marvel Rising: Chasing Ghosts’ in Jan. 2019. She voiced the role of Ironheart/Riri Williams in ‘Marvel Rising: Heart of Iron.’ Sofia also launched her own production company, AIFOS Productions, in March 2019.
