Takaya Kuroda is a Japanese actor, voice actor, and singer who is known for his voiceover roles like Yaiba in GoGo Sentai Boukenger and Kazuma Kiryu in the Yakuza series. Over the years, he has also been a part of a number of other projects including Fafner of the Azure Right of Left, Wolf’s Rain, Battle B-Daman, R.O.D the TV, Burst Angel, Transformers: Cybertron, Mai Otome, Black Cat, Black Lagoon, Black Blood Brothers, Chocotto Sister, The Tower of Druaga: The Aegis of Uruk, Himitsu – Top Secret, Natsume’s Book of Friends, Blade of the Immortal, The Girl Who Leapt Through Space, Fairy Tail, Highschool of the Dead, Hyouge Mono, Horizon on the Middle of Nowhere, Invaders of the Rokujyōma!?, Joker Game, Yo-kai Watch Shadowside: Oni-ō no Fukkatsu, 91 Days, Yo-kai Watch Shadowside, Uma Musume Pretty Derby, Appleseed Ex Machina, and Farewell, My Dear Cramer.

Takaya Kuroda Facts

  • He has been the lead singer of the rock band Takaya Kuroda & Goodfellas.
  • His voice resembles the voice of late actor Hirotaka Suzuoki and due to this, Takaya was chosen to replace Suzuoki as the voice of Starscream in Transformers: Cybertron when Suzuoki’s health deteriorated.
  • Takaya Kuroda has also done voiceover work for several video games including Tales of Symphonia, Romancing SaGa: Minstrel Song, Fatal Frame III: The Tormented, Ryū ga Gotoku Kenzan!, Marvel vs Capcom 3, Everybody’s Golf 6, Etrian Odyssey Untold: The Millennium Girl, and Fallout 4.
