Big Sean Hits Coachella &amp; The White House In His Latest Weekend Recap Vlog

In case you were remiss as to what Big Sean was up to last weekend, never fear, his ‘Weekend Recap’ blog is here. Chronicling the Detroit player’s super-busy couple of days last weekend, the 28th episode documents Sean’s appearance at Weekend 2 of Coachella as well as his appearance at The White House last Easter Sunday.

Complete with clips of him chopping it up with the Smith kids and dapping it up with Kid Cudi, Big Sean’s energetic Coachella set included renditions of his verses on “Mercy” and “All Me.” Sean Don also becomes the “first rapper to rap” at The White House during his performance with Ariana Grande – who cries when she meets Jim Carrey – at the 2014 Easter Egg Roll. And he tops it all off by heading back Los Angeles for an Aura Gold photoshoot. All in a weekend’s work.
