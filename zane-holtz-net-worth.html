Zane Holtz’s Million Dollars Net Worth!

Zane Holtz holds a net worth of $2 million as of 2020. He gathered that fortune from his career as an actor and model.

Holtz started his career as a model. He started to model at the early age of five. Besides, he did his first-ever commercial on his 10th birthday.

As for the acting career of the actor, he started as a television actor. Well, at first, he worked in the 2001 television series called CSI: Crime Scene Investigation as Dylan Buckley. After that, he worked in numerous television series like Judging Amy, Make It or Break It, and Cold Case, among others.

Holtz also tried his luck in the film industry. In 2003, he debuted in the film industry after working in the film Holes as Louis/Barf Bag. Lead-In From Dusk till Dawn: The Series

Among all television works of Zane, he is mostly recognized for his lead role in the television series, From Dusk till Dawn: The Series. Well, he portrayed the role of Richie Gecko after joining the cast of the series in 2014.

The actor portrayed the role of Richie in all 30 episodes of the drama for which he received a nomination for the Fangoria Chainsaw Award for Best TV Supporting Actor. Additionally, the series also features actors like Eiza Gonzalez, Madison Davenport, and Wilmer Valderrama.
